5. Market Risks

Financial markets are subject to various risks including but not limited to:

  • Market Risk: Prices can move against your position
  • Volatility Risk: Markets can experience sudden price swings
  • Liquidity Risk: You may not be able to exit positions when desired
  • Leverage Risk: Leveraged trading can amplify both gains and losses
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations can affect returns
  • Political Risk: Government policies can impact markets
  • Economic Risk: Economic events can cause market disruption

6. Forex Trading Risks

Foreign exchange (forex) trading carries specific risks:

  • High volatility and unpredictable price movements
  • Leverage can result in losses exceeding initial investment
  • 24-hour market exposure to global events
  • Currency fluctuations affect profit and loss
  • Interest rate changes impact currency values
  • Political instability can cause sudden market movements

7. Cryptocurrency Risks

Cryptocurrency investments involve additional risks:

  • Extreme price volatility and market speculation
  • Regulatory uncertainty and potential restrictions
  • Technology risks and potential system failures
  • Limited liquidity in some markets
  • Security risks including hacking and theft
  • Lack of investor protection in many jurisdictions
